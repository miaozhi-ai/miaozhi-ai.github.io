Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2025

CHENGDU MIAOZHI TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in our mobile applications and related services ("Services").

1. We Do Not Collect Personal Information

Our applications do not require you to provide any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number) to use the core features. We do not store or collect any sensitive personal data on our own servers.

2. Use of Third-Party SDKs

We may integrate third-party SDKs in our applications for the purposes of analytics, advertising, and crash reporting. These SDKs may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Language settings and country/region
  • App usage behavior (e.g. clicks, session duration)
  • Advertising ID (e.g. IDFA or GAID)
  • IP address (for approximate location)

Examples of such third-party services include (but are not limited to):

  • Google AdMob (for in-app ads)
  • Firebase / Google Analytics (for usage statistics)
  • Facebook Audience Network
  • Appsflyer, Adjust or other attribution tools

3. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

4. Data Security

We do not store any personal information on our servers. However, we encourage users to be aware of the data collection practices of third-party SDKs. We take reasonable technical measures to ensure the security of our applications.

5. Data Retention

As we do not collect personal data directly, we do not retain any such information. Third-party providers may retain certain data as per their own retention policies.

6. Your Choices

You can manage your privacy preferences through your device settings, such as:

  • Resetting your advertising ID
  • Limiting ad tracking
  • Opting out of personalized ads
  • Disabling analytics (if available in settings)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
