Children's Addiction Prevention Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2025

At CHENGDU MIAOZHI TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. (“we”, “us”, or “our”), we are committed to ensuring that our applications are safe and suitable for children. This Children's Addiction Prevention Policy explains how we address excessive usage and promote healthy digital habits.

1. Promoting Healthy Usage

We encourage balanced and healthy use of our applications. Our products are designed to provide positive, educational, and entertaining experiences without encouraging excessive or prolonged usage.

2. Usage Time Limits

Where applicable, we provide features that allow parents or guardians to manage and limit children’s usage time, helping prevent overuse.

3. Periodic Break Reminders

Our applications may include reminders encouraging users to take regular breaks after extended periods of use.

4. Parental Controls

We support parental control mechanisms such as restricting access, managing usage duration, and disabling certain features where supported by the platform.

5. No Targeting of Children Under 13

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be promptly deleted.

6. Encouraging Offline Activities

We encourage children to balance screen time with offline activities, including physical exercise, outdoor play, and social interaction.

儿童防沉迷政策

最后更新日期：2025年7月10日

成都妙至科技有限公司（以下简称“我们”）高度重视儿童的身心健康， 致力于为儿童提供安全、健康、积极的数字产品体验。 本儿童防沉迷政策说明我们在防止过度使用方面所采取的措施。

1. 提倡健康使用

我们倡导合理、适度地使用应用产品，不鼓励长时间或不当使用， 并在产品设计中融入健康使用理念。

2. 使用时间管理

在适用情况下，我们支持家长通过系统或应用功能对儿童的使用时间进行管理， 帮助防止沉迷。

3. 定期休息提示

应用可能在连续使用一段时间后提示用户进行休息， 以降低长时间使用带来的风险。

4. 家长控制支持

我们支持平台提供的家长控制功能，包括限制使用时长、 管理访问权限及关闭部分功能等。

5. 不面向13岁以下儿童

本应用不以13岁以下儿童为目标用户。 若发现无意中收集了13岁以下儿童的个人信息， 我们将立即删除相关数据。

6. 鼓励线下活动

我们鼓励儿童将数字娱乐与现实生活相结合， 多参与户外活动、体育锻炼和社交互动。
