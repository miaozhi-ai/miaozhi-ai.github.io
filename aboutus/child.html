Children's Addiction Prevention Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2025

At CHENGDU MIAOZHI TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. ("we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to ensuring that our applications are safe and suitable for children. This Children's Addiction Prevention Policy explains how we address concerns regarding excessive use and addiction prevention in our mobile applications.

1. We Promote Healthy Usage

We believe in promoting a balanced and healthy usage of our applications. We do not encourage excessive use, and we design our apps to provide fun and educational experiences in a way that promotes well-being.

2. Usage Time Limits

We provide users with the ability to set time limits on usage. This feature allows parents and guardians to manage their child's screen time and prevent prolonged use of the app.

3. Periodic Breaks

Our applications are designed to encourage regular breaks. We prompt users to take breaks after prolonged usage to reduce the risk of screen addiction.

4. Parental Controls

We provide parental control features that allow parents to monitor and control their child's usage of the app. This includes features like disabling in-app purchases, setting time restrictions, and monitoring usage behavior.

5. No Targeting of Children Under 13

Our apps are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information immediately.

6. Encouraging Offline Activities

We encourage children to balance their screen time with offline activities. Our apps may suggest fun outdoor activities and encourage physical play to ensure a healthy lifestyle.

儿童防沉迷政策

最后更新日期：2025年7月10日

成都妙至科技有限公司（“我们”，“我们公司”或“我们的”）致力于确保我们的应用程序对儿童既安全又适宜。本儿童防沉迷政策解释了我们如何处理关于过度使用和防止沉迷的问题。

1. 我们提倡健康使用

我们倡导平衡和健康使用我们的应用程序。我们不会鼓励过度使用，且我们设计的应用程序旨在提供有趣且富有教育意义的体验，促进用户的身心健康。

2. 使用时间限制

我们为用户提供设置使用时间限制的功能。家长和监护人可以通过此功能管理孩子的屏幕时间，防止应用程序过度使用。

3. 定期休息

我们的应用程序设计了定期休息的功能。我们会提示用户在长时间使用后休息，以减少屏幕沉迷的风险。

4. 家长控制功能

我们提供家长控制功能，允许家长监控和控制孩子使用应用程序的行为。这包括禁用应用内购买、设置时间限制和监控使用行为等功能。

5. 不针对13岁以下儿童

我们的应用程序不针对13岁以下儿童。如果我们发现收集了13岁以下儿童的个人信息，我们会立即采取措施删除该信息。

6. 鼓励线下活动

我们鼓励孩子将屏幕时间与线下活动相结合。我们的应用程序可能会建议有趣的户外活动，并鼓励进行体育锻炼，以确保健康的生活方式。
